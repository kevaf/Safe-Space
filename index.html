WELCOME TO SAFE SPACE

Research dhows that 60% of sexual abuse cases are not reported in Kenya.


Both women and men who are sexually abused fear going to the relevant authorities to report bad deeds done to them.


In a effort to stem out stigmatization an help out those in need Safe-Space helps your report sexual assult crimes remotely using your device hence help cure the bad menace in our country.


Safe-Space also plays a big role in educating the public on effects of sexual assault and we hope to give victims solutions to their problems.

ABOUT US

Safe-Space is a non profit organization formed in 2007 with the aim of helping out the vulnerable.


Our main concern is sexual abuse on both genders. We aim at improving the awareness on sexual health and educate people on the consequences of sexual assault


We also help nab sexual aggressors and make the face the full warth of the law


We also offer guidance and counselling to victims of sexual assault.
